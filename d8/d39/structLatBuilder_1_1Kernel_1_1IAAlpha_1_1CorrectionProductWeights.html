Detailed Description

This class mimicks the LatticeTester::ProductWeights class.

It is used to interlace weights of dimension \(s\) into weights of dimension \( d s \). See Corollary 2. and Remark 1. of [rGOD13a]. In the case of the \(\B_{d, \gamma, (1)}\), the weights are even equal to 1 for all the interlaced components. This corresponds to \(\delta_l \) in the interlaced weights (see LatBuilder::Interlaced::IPDWeights).
