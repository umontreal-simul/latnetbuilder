Computes lacunary indices by groups of \(s\), spaced apart by \(2^w\).

If \(w=0\), this is the case of non-lacunary indices. If MinDim is smaller than Order, it is reset to Order + 1. Returns true in the lacunary case, and false otherwise.

References LatticeTester::CreateVect(), LatticeTester::DeleteVect(), and LatticeTester::power2().
